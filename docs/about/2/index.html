About

FoxGibbon is a carefully curated platform for design and creative inspiration featuring design news, psychology and ideas.

Founded by Thomas Mott, I work as a specialist user-experiece / user-interface designer and front-end developer. I have a strong interest in accessibility, user testing, design psychology, ethical design, and work on a lot of side projects.

I'm located in Bristol, England. I have worked as a freelance consultant in the past and am always interested in impactful opportunities. This can be from accessibility audits, to wireframing or coding front-end components.
